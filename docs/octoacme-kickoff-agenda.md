# OctoAcme — Kickoff Meeting Agenda Template

## Purpose
Provide a reusable agenda for project kickoff meetings to ensure all critical topics are covered, expectations are aligned, and the team starts execution with a shared understanding. See [Project Planning](octoacme-project-planning.md) for planning process guidance.

## How to use
1. Copy this agenda and populate the placeholders before the meeting.
2. Share with all attendees at least 24 hours in advance.
3. Assign a facilitator (typically the PM) and a note-taker.
4. Capture outputs in the project repo alongside the Project One-pager.

### 1. Welcome & Introductions (5 min)
- Brief team introductions (name and role)
- Meeting purpose: align on goals, scope, and ways of working

### 2. Project Overview (10 min)
- Problem statement and business context
- Goals and success metrics (from Project One-pager)
- Out-of-scope items (what we are NOT doing)

### 3. Roles & Responsibilities (5 min)
- Confirm who is in each role (PM, PdM, Lead Dev, QA, Stakeholders)
- Identify primary contacts for decisions and escalations
- Reference: [Roles and Personas](octoacme-roles-and-personas.md)

### 4. Scope & Milestones (10 min)
- Walk through the high-level roadmap and key milestones
- Confirm timeline constraints and hard deadlines
- Identify known dependencies (internal and external)

### 5. Ways of Working (10 min)
- Sprint length and ceremonies (standup, planning, review, retro)
- Communication channels (Slack/Teams, email, GitHub)
- PR review and merge policies
- Definition of Done: [DoD Template](octoacme-definition-of-done.md)

### 6. Risks & Open Questions (10 min)
- Walk through initial risk list from the Project One-pager
- Open floor for team risks and concerns
- Capture in: [Risk Register](octoacme-risk-register-template.md)

### 7. Next Steps & Actions (5 min)
- Confirm immediate next actions, owners, and due dates
- Schedule first sprint planning / first standup
- Where to find project artifacts (repo, board, docs)

---

## Post-Kickoff Checklist
- [ ] Meeting notes shared with all attendees within 24 hours
- [ ] Action items logged with owners and due dates
- [ ] Project board created / updated with initial backlog
- [ ] Risk register populated with items from the session
- [ ] Communication cadence confirmed and calendar invites sent

## Owner & Accountability
- **Facilitated by:** Project Manager (PM)
- **Notes captured by:** Designated note-taker
- **Roles reference:** See [Roles and Personas](octoacme-roles-and-personas.md)
